Ocean Dos

Ocean Dos, Playa Blanca Resort

Ocean II, is found within the beautiful Playa Blanca Resort, on the Pacific Coast of Panama. This building is the first of eight towers that will be constructed. The Model Apartment which E+A Studio completed will be the start of an advertisement campaign that will push the sale of all eight towers. Our ultimate goal for this Model Apartment was to make it "photo-ready". The bones of this space had been established when we arrived, although it desperately lacked character. Within two weeks we were able to transform this three bedroom beach front apartment. The clients' goals were exceeded and under budget. E+A Studio is currently working on the design of the Lobby and Pool Deck. Images coming soon.
