Bijao Penthouse

Bijao Penthouse, Santa Clara

This private residence is located within the development of Bijao on the Pacific Coast near Santa Clara. The homeowners who had recently retired, were seeking an elegant home, yet comfortable and airy. Relaxed details along with a warm color scheme were intertwined with the clients' existing high-end furniture. Each of the three bedrooms were carefully designed to have their own unique style. Our clients were thrilled with the final product. They will enjoy the view of the ocean breeze against their off-white sheers for years to come.
