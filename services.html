Design Services

E + A Studio offers a wide variety of services, from project inspiration to fruition.

We are a full service interior design firm that specializes in creating custom new construction residential interiors ranging from 5,000 to 30,000 square feet. Our experience, passion for excellence, and ability to execute has given us not only the reputation as the area’s top interior design firm, but has also put us on the international stage for designing custom projects for homes in Costa Rica, Mexico and the U.S.

What We Do

Our team understands that building a new home is not only a significant financial investment, but a major life milestone that deserves to be celebrated. It is our goal to honor a client’s life achievement by providing unparalleled design services that are proven to help navigate the many challenges of building a custom new home.
