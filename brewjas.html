Brewjas Pub

Brewjas Pub, La Chorrera, Panama

This quaint beer pub is the first of its kind in the town of La Chorrera, located 35 minutes from Panama City, Panama. It was very important to the owners that this space felt like an extension of your living room. A space where you can have entertaining conversation in a warm atmosphere over a pint of great craft beer. To achieve this feeling, wood was used throughout the space. Large wood planks line the walls, a variety of wood species were used for the bar front and custom rustic wood furniture was designed. Special touches such as a concrete bar top, industrial light fixtures and artwork from a local graffiti artist make the interiors truly special.
