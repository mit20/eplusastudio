La Cascara TV

La Cascara Office and Television Studio, Panamá City - Panamá

La Cascara is the longest running comedy show airing in Panamá City. The clients are a group of incredibly creative and talented individuals. Every aspect of this project was thought out as a team, it was a true collaboration. The project consist of the renovation of the existing offices along with a new building that contains a state-of-the-art television studio. The clients vision of a loft style space were carried out in the exposed concrete, brick and stainless steel details throughout. Special touches such as teak wood floors, graffiti murals and vintage style furniture can be also be found.
